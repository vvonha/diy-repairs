Basic Toolkit: Screwdrivers, hammer, pliers, adjustable wrench, tape measure, level, drill, utility knife, flashlight, duct tape, WD-40

Plumbing

Unclog drain: Try plunger first. Then baking soda + vinegar (1/2 cup each), wait 30 min, hot water flush.
Running toilet: Usually the flapper. $5 part, 10 minutes. Lift tank lid, check if flapper seals.
Leaky faucet: Replace washer or cartridge. Turn off water first. YouTube your specific model.

Walls & Doors

Patch small holes: Spackle, let dry, sand smooth, paint. Under $10.
Fix squeaky door: WD-40 on hinges. Wipe excess.
Tighten loose doorknob: Find setscrew on side, tighten with Allen wrench.
Sticky door: Mark where it sticks, sand or plane that spot.

Electrical (Simple)

Replace outlet cover: Unscrew, swap, done. Power can stay on.
Change light fixture: Turn OFF breaker. Photo old wiring. Match colors. Ground wire to green screw.
Reset tripped breaker: Flip fully OFF, then ON. If it keeps tripping, call electrician.

Other Common Fixes

Squeaky floor: Sprinkle talcum powder in cracks. Work it in.
Stuck window: Score paint seal with utility knife. Tap frame gently.
Loose cabinet hinge: Remove screw, fill hole with toothpick + wood glue, reinsert screw.
Clean garbage disposal: Ice cubes + salt, run disposal. Then lemon for smell.
